ASK FOR AN OUTLINE · 8 MIN
Get the structure.
Choose ONE of Claude's angles (or — even better — one of YOUR original 5 ideas). Then ask Claude to outline it.
📋 COPY AND FILL IN
Give me a 5-paragraph outline for a 400-word expository essay arguing that [YOUR THESIS]. For each paragraph, give me: (a) topic sentence, (b) 2 supporting points, (c) a transition to the next paragraph.
Paste the outline into your Journal under: Entry 4 — Section 3 — AI Outline.
5
CRITICAL EVALUATION · 8 MIN
Mark up the outline.
Read Claude's outline carefully. Use 3 colors (or 3 symbols) to mark every line.
✓
KEEP
Strong, specific, true to what you want to say.
✎
MODIFY
Useful but not quite right. Note WHAT you'd change.
✗
REJECT
Generic, off-topic, factually shaky, or not your voice. Note WHY.
⚡
THE TEST
If you marked everything KEEP, you didn't push back hard enough. A good editor rejects something. So should you.
6
PAIR CHECK · 5 MIN
Trade journals.
Find your partner. Read each other's marked-up outlines. Ask: Did they push back enough? Did they accept too much? Give one specific suggestion.
