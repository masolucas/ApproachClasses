YOUR FIVE STEPS

Take it one move at a time.

1
Open Claude.
Go to claude.ai on your laptop. Make sure you're logged in. You should see a chat box ready for input.
2
Say hello — your way.
Type a hello message in your own words. It can be short. Press Enter and read what Claude says back.
3
Ask the question that matters.
Now copy the prompt below and paste it into Claude.
📋 PROMPT TO COPY
I'm a student in a writing class. We're starting to use AI as a writing tool today. In two short paragraphs: what are you genuinely good at for writing, and what are you genuinely bad at? Be honest — don't oversell yourself.
4
Save the conversation.
Copy the entire exchange (your prompt + Claude's response) into your Process Journal. Use this heading:

Entry 4 — Section 1 — Meet Your AI Partner
5
Compare with a partner.
Find the person sitting next to you. Look at each other's responses from Claude. Did you get the same answer? Different? Discuss for 2 minutes.
⚡
NOTICE THIS
AI gives slightly different answers every time — even to the exact same prompt. This is important. Two students with the same prompt will not get the same essay. That can be useful (variety) and dangerous (you can't rely on AI being consistent).
