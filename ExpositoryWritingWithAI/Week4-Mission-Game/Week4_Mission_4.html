IF YOU GET STUCK

You can ask Claude — but only certain things.

✓
OK TO ASK
  • "What's a transition word for contrast here?"asks for an option, not a sentence
  • "What's a synonym for [word]?"asks for vocabulary, not a sentence
  • "Is my topic sentence in this paragraph clear?"asks for feedback, not a rewrite
  • "Does this paragraph make sense?"asks for evaluation, not editing
✗
NOT OK TO ASK
  • "Write paragraph 2 for me."AI writes sentences = you copy AI
  • "Rewrite this in better English."AI replaces your voice with theirs
  • "Polish my whole essay."AI takes over the whole text
  • "Make this paragraph longer."AI fills in your thinking
