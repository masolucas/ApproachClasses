PART 1 · RUN THE PROMPTS

Four prompts. Run them in order.

Type each prompt into Claude. Read the response together. Make a quick note of what you observed before moving to the next.

1
VAGUE
Almost no context
📋 PROMPT
Write something about education.
⚡ WATCH FOR
How generic does it get? Does it pick a direction on its own — or stay neutral?
2
SPECIFIC
Audience and constraints
📋 PROMPT
Write a 100-word paragraph for Brazilian high school students about why studying English helps their future career. Use a friendly, motivating tone.
⚡ WATCH FOR
How much better does AI do when you give it audience + length + tone? Compare to Prompt 1.
3
PERSONAL
Asks for things AI can't know
📋 PROMPT
Write a 100-word paragraph about my grandmother's recipe for feijoada. Include the secret ingredient she always added.
⚡ WATCH FOR
Did AI invent a "secret ingredient" it has no way of knowing? This is hallucination — and it sounds confident.
4
CURRENT FACTS
Asks about right now
📋 PROMPT
What were the top 3 news stories in São Paulo this week? Be specific — give me dates and headlines.
⚡ WATCH FOR
Did AI invent news stories? Refuse? Give vague answers? AI does not know what's happening today.
