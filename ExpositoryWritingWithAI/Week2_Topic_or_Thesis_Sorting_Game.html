Can you tell the difference?

You'll see 12 statements one at a time. For each one, decide: is it just a topic, a weak thesis, or a strong thesis?

How to classify

T
Topic — Describes a subject but makes no claim. You can't agree or disagree with it.
W
Weak Thesis — Makes a claim, but it's vague, obvious, or tries to cover "both sides."
S
Strong Thesis — Makes a specific, debatable claim and tells the reader what the argument will be.
